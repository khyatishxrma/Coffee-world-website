Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp instant coffee
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Whip coffee, sugar, and water until fluffy.
  2. Pour milk into glass.
  3. Top with whipped coffee and serve.
