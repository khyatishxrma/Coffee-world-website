Ingredients

  • 18g finely ground coffee
  • Hot water (90-96°C)

Instructions

  1. Place coffee into the espresso machine portafilter.
  2. Tamp it evenly and firmly.
  3. Lock the portafilter and start the shot.
  4. Extract for about 25–30 seconds.
  5. Serve in a demitasse cup and enjoy!

Tip: A good espresso has a thick crema on top ☁️
