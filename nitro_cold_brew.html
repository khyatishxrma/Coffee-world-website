Ingredients

  • Cold brew concentrate
  • Nitrogen (infused via tap or canister)

Instructions

  1. Chill the cold brew.
  2. Infuse with nitrogen using a keg or nitro canister.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy the foamy top!

Tip: Creamy and bubbly without milk!
