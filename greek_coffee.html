Ingredients

  • Greek coffee
  • Cold water
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a briki.
  2. Heat slowly and let foam rise (do not boil).
  3. Pour into a demitasse cup with foam on top.

Tip: Served with a glass of cold water and a smile 😄
