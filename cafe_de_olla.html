Ingredients

  • Ground coffee
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Piloncillo (or brown sugar)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Boil water with cinnamon and piloncillo.
  2. Add coffee and simmer briefly.
  3. Strain and serve hot.

Tip: A warm Mexican hug in a cup ☕❤️
