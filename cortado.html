Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Equal amount of steamed milk

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Add an equal amount of steamed milk.
  3. Serve in a small glass.

Tip: Balanced and less foamy than a cappuccino.
