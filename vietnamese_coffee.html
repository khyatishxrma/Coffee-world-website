Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Vietnamese coffee
  • 2 tbsp condensed milk
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. Place condensed milk in the cup.
  2. Use phin filter to brew coffee over it.
  3. Stir and enjoy!

Tip: Strong, sweet, and perfect for hot or iced versions 🇻🇳
