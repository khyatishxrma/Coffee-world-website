Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Hot water

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Add hot water to the espresso (1:2 ratio).
  3. Serve hot and enjoy.

Tip: Americano is less intense than espresso but still strong!
