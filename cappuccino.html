Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Foamed milk

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Steam milk and create foam.
  3. Pour espresso into a cup, add steamed milk, and top with foam.

Tip: Dust with cocoa powder for extra charm.
