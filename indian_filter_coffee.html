Ingredients

  • Ground coffee
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Brew decoction using a filter.
  2. Boil milk and mix with decoction.
  3. Add sugar to taste.
  4. Pour back and forth for froth!

Tip: Also called 'kaapi' in South India!
