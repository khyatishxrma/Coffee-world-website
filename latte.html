Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 200ml steamed milk
  • Foamed milk for topping

Instructions

  1. Pull a shot of espresso into a cup.
  2. Add steamed milk.
  3. Top with a light layer of foamed milk.

Tip: Lattes are perfect canvas for latte art ☕🎨
