Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Steamed milk

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Add microfoam steamed milk (less foam than a latte).
  3. Serve in a small cup.

Tip: A flat white is strong, smooth, and creamy.
