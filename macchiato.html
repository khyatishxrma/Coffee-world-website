Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1-2 teaspoons foamed milk

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Spoon a dollop of foamed milk on top.
  3. Serve immediately.

Tip: ‘Macchiato’ means ‘stained’ in Italian ☕
