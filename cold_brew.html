Ingredients

  • Coarsely ground coffee
  • Cold water

Instructions

  1. Mix coffee and water (1:4 ratio).
  2. Let steep in the fridge for 12–24 hours.
  3. Strain and serve over ice.

Tip: Less acidic and super refreshing!
