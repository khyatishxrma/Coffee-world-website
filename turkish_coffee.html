Ingredients

  • Finely ground Turkish coffee
  • Water
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix water and coffee (and sugar if needed) in a cezve.
  2. Heat slowly without stirring.
  3. Pour foam into cup, reheat remainder, pour again.

Tip: Don't stir after it’s poured — grounds settle at the bottom!
