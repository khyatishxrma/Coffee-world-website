Ingredients

  • Hot coffee
  • Irish whiskey
  • Brown sugar
  • Whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Mix hot coffee with whiskey and sugar.
  2. Stir until dissolved.
  3. Float whipped cream on top.

Tip: Don’t stir the cream — sip through it like a boss 😌
