Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • Steamed milk
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add chocolate syrup into a mug.
  2. Pour in a shot of espresso.
  3. Add steamed milk and stir well.
  4. Top with whipped cream if desired.

Tip: Great for coffee + chocolate lovers! 🍫
